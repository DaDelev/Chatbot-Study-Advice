Advice and Services

I don't know what to study...

Would you like to find out more about the degree programs on offer at the University of Mannheim? Are you unsure about whether studying is right for you? Do you need help with choosing a degree program? You can make an appointment at the Student Advising Center to discuss these, and similar, questions.
