Media Technolocy

The lecture halls and seminar rooms of the University of Mannheim are equipped with various media technology devices. The media technology department is responsible for these rooms, maintains and cares for the multimedia equipment and makes sure that the equipment is constantly updated and new equipment is added to the collection.

We also support you in realizing your multi-media projects: You can borrow professional technical equipment from us. You can also create or edit digital media in our Media Editing Room.
