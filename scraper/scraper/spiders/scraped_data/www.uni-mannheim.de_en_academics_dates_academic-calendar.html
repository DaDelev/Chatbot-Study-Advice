Academic Calendar

The University of Mannheim has aligned its academic calendar with the international academic year. This means that lectures begin in September in the fall semester, and in February in the spring semester. The orientation weeks for first-year students and exchange students always take place in the week before the lecture period begins.

Future Academic Calendars

  • Dates for the Fall Semester 2023

    Semester: 1 August 2023 – 31 January 2024
    Re-registration: 1 May 2023 – 15 June 2023
    Lecture period: 4 September 2023 – 8 December 2023
    Examination period: 9 December 2023 – 22 December 2023
    Alternate examination dates: 3 February 2024 – 10 February 2024

  • Dates for the Spring Semester 2024

    Semester: 1 February 2024 – 31 July 2024
    Re-registration: 15 October 2023 – 1 December 2023
    Lecture period: 12 February 2024 – 31 May 2024
    Easter break: 25 March 2024 – 5 April 2024
    Examination period: 3 June 2024 – 15 June 2024
    Alternate examination dates: 24 August 2024 – 31 August 2024

  • Dates for the Fall Semester 2024

    Semester: 1 August 2024 – 31 January 2025
    Re-registration: 1 May 2024 – 15 June 2024
    Lecture period: 2 September 2024 – 6 December 2024
    Examination period: 9 December 2024 – 21 December 2024
    Alternate examination dates: 1 February 2025 – 8 February 2025

  • Dates for the Spring Semester 2025

    Semester: 1 February 2025 – 31 July 2025
    Re-registration: 15 October 2024 – 1 December 2024
    Lecture period: 10 February 2025 – 30 May 2025
    Easter break: 14 April 2025 – 25 April 2025

    Alternate examination dates: 23 August 2025 – 30 August 2025

  • Dates for the Fall Semester 2025

    Semester: 1 August 2025 – 31 January 2026
